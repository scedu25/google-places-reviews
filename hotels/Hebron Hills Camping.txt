Text: We camped here over Labor Day weekend. The staff are very friendly as well as all the other campers that we met. The sites were not huge. The campground was very quiet, and was a great place to take a family. Great playground for the kids and the shower/bathroom was clean. The cost to camp was inexpensive and they had full hookups available. We will camp here again in the future.
