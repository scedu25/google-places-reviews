Grand Central Hotel
Rating: 3
Text: This historic 11- room hotel is in the heart of downtown Auburn, NE, just minutes away from Cooper Nuclear Station at Brownville, Peru State College and Indian Cave State Park in Southeast Nebraska. It is a work in progress, with some of the rooms already being completed and others in the process of being remodeled. Currently it is considered an extended stay hotel, with a minimum of 3 nights. There are various rooms available in the hotel, from single rooms to large kitchenettes featuring queen size beds. Pets and children under 12 are not allowed. The hotel is reasonable priced and comfortably furnished.
