Text: This is probably the best cottage you will find in the vicinity of Berea. It creates a unique feeling of home and puts you at ease, because of it's secluded location within the lush greenery, nicely decorated, spacious rooms and warm, bright colors of the walls inspiring energy and positive thoughts. It is equipped with all the modern conveniences and is located within walking distance of the Berea College Square and it's many shops and restaurants. This cottage is a wonderful place for any kind of trip through the town that will allow you to rest well during your stay.
