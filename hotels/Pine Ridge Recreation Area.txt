Text: Nice secluded campground that's only a twenty minute drive from Columbia. Sites are first come, first serve, but are free. Campground host is friendly as well. Unlike a lot of other campgrounds, these sites are packed in right next to each other. There is well over a hundred feet between this campsite and the next. Definitely a hidden jewel of Mid-MO if you like to camp.
