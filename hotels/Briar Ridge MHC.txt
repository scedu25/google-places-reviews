Text: Briar Ridge is located in a rural setting on the east side of Terre Haute, IN just 1 mile west of Seelyville and 7 miles west of Brazil. Snuggled off of US Highway 40 in an area known as the Wabash Valley. Briar Ridge is a small family oriented community that offers you the best of country living but minutes from Indiana State University and Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology. Fast food and fine dining along with many churches and schools can be found nearby. When you’re ready for a weekend getaway, Indianapolis is only a 50 minute drive away.
