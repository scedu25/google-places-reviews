Text: On 8-19-17, Franchesco’s catered my wedding at DC Estate Winery. Planning from start to finish was a 5-star experience. First off, this is a locally owned and operated business, and the entire family takes a lot of pride in what they do. I worked with Michelle for planning the big day, and she was very responsive, very helpful, and very thorough in all of the details. It was obvious they had catered many large parties before because they didn’t miss a beat. And although this is “what they do”, my experience seemed very personalized. We served 130 guests a delicious surf-n-turf with a small filet and orange roughly. I told my (at the time) fiancé that the one thing I wish for most at our wedding was for everyone to enjoy the food (because let’s face it, wedding food is usually very mediocre). Bottom line… My husband and I truly enjoyed working with this family, and we felt great giving them our business. I often joke to people that the 1 problem with our wedding is that it’s unfortunately only a 1 time event. Because if I could do it all over again, I’d do it in a heartbeat, and Franchesco’s would be one of the first calls I’d make!
